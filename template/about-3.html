What is RMS?

The RMS mission is to create a more resilient and sustainable global society through a better understanding of catastrophic events. From earthquakes, hurricanes, and floods, to terrorism and infectious disease.

About Me

My background is in front-end development, but over the past 5 years, I have focused on helping scale product design teams integrating an Agile Design environment [trying my hand at implementing this agile process at RMS can be found here.

Often designers are mistakenly thrown toward decorative solutions alone without identifying the correct problem. It's important to mention that product design is not artwork, but rather a craft that is built first and foremost around solving the right problems. In today's practice of design it is not enough to create answers with pixel perfect Sketch files. Design solutions need lots of context, rationale, and results. Over the course of my career, I have been outcome driven and extremely results oriented. In my free time, I enjoy photography, learning new technologies and writing about UX. Some of my recent articles can be seen on UX Collective.

Currently, I work for a software company called RMS (Risk Management Solutions) where I wear many hats in the product design realm. My work focuses primarily on all RMS (one) product design initiatives including conceptual exploration sketching, white-boarding, high (and Low) fidelity wireframes and pixel-perfect UI prototypes.
