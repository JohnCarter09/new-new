About Me

Originally from Arizona, I moved to Colorado in 2009. I was stationed in Colorado Springs at Fort Carson where I was enlisted in the United States Army. Honorably discharged in 2012, I enrolled in an International Business Program at The University Of Colorado where I successfully finished my bachelor's degree in 2 & 1/2 years.

Currently, I work for a software company called RMS (Risk Management Solutions) where I wear many hats in the UX realm. My work focuses primarily on all RMS (one) product design initiatives including conceptual exploration sketching, white-boarding, high (and Low) fidelity wireframes and pixel-perfect UI prototypes.

Over the course of my career I have worked in very diverse and inclusive environments. Everything from large software compaines, to small nimble start-ups. I am passionate about owning effective user experiences that are not only delightful, but solve the right problems. In my free time, I enjoy photography, learning new technologies and writing about UX. Some of my recent articles can be seen on UX Collective.
