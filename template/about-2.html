About Me

For over half a decade, I have been focused on honing in my design skills, helping product design teams operate at scale, and delivering valuable software.

Often designers mistakenly throw themselves at a decorative solution alone without identifying the correct problem. It's important to mention that product design is not artwork, but rather a craft that is built first and foremost around solving the right problems. In today's practice of design it is not enough to create answers with pixel perfect Sketch files. Design solutions need lots of context, rationale, and results.


Over the course of my career I have worked in very diverse and inclusive environments. Everything from large software compaines, to small nimble start-ups. All the while being outcome driven and extremely results oriented. In my free time, I enjoy photography and writing about design. Some of my recent articles can be seen on UX Collective.

Right now, My work focuses primarily on all RMS (one) product design initiatives including conceptual exploration sketching, white-boarding, high (and Low) fidelity wireframes and pixel-perfect UI prototypes. I love process, and have been dabbling in the DesignOps territory lately.
